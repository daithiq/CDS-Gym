Covid-19

Covid-19 has impacted all of our lives but it is now time to get back on track, and there is no better place to start than the gym. Taking care of our mental and physical health has never been more important and we promise to make your visit as safe as possible so you can focus on yourself and your health.

What we are doing

Your safety is our number one priority. Keeping you healthy and keeping the gym open is what we are fully focused on. We have employed additional team members to ensure the gym is kept clean and sanatized throughtout the day. We are putting every measure in place to protect you so that you don't have to worry about it while you are with us. In addition to extra staff, we have sanatizing stations throughout the gym for peace of mind.

How you can help

While we will make every effort to protect you, we also need your help. If you are feel as though you may be developing symptons of covid or have come into close contact with someone who has covid we would ask you to refrain from attending the gym. We will be more than happy to welcome you back once aftwerwards.
